This data is significant because it gives us an idea of the array of fuel sources we use in a variety of sectors. Regulated data is protected by different laws enacted nationally and internationally. In addition, I learned about public and restricted (private) data. Public data is data that anyone has access to; people are able to freely use and distribute the data. Restricted data, however, is not available for public use or viewing. Usually this data is confidential and viewership is limited to only certain people.
